GonogoKos compile-time links kOS (kOS.dll / kOS.Safe.dll) and, from P1
onward, Harmony (0Harmony.dll) for one observe-only ScreenBuffer.Print
postfix. It reads kOS's public API in-process — no arm's-length reflection,
no loopback telnet. See local_docs/telemetry-mod/kos-migration-spec.md §8.

gonogo as a whole is MIT-licensed (repository root LICENSE). GonogoKos is
the deliberate exception: it is the only assembly in the repository that
links a copyleft dependency, and it is a leaf — nothing references it, so
its GPL obligations propagate to nothing else. The MIT-licensed Sitrep.*
assemblies it links are GPL-compatible in that direction, and GonogoKos
ships as its own CKAN package in its own GameData folder.
