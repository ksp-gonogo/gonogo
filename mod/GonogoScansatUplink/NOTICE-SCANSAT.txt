GonogoScansatUplink reads runtime data from SCANsat (https://github.com/S-C-A-N/SCANsat)
through its public API and replicates several small public-input formulas from
its source (getFOV, getElevation, getBiomeIndex conventions - see
local_docs/telemetry-mod/scansat-migration-spec.md §0D/§0E). SCANsat is
