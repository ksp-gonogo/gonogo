GonogoActionGroupsExtendedUplink integrates with Action Groups Extended
(AGX, https://github.com/linuxgurugamer/AGExt) entirely at ARM'S LENGTH: it
reads AGX's runtime state through its PUBLIC static API
(ActionGroupsExtended.AGExtExternal) by reflection ONLY. It does NOT link the
AGExt assembly at build time, does NOT derive from any AGExt type, and
bundles NO AGExt source or assets.
