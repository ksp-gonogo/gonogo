GonogoRealAntennasUplink integrates with RealAntennas
(https://github.com/kerbalnet/RealAntennas) entirely at ARM'S LENGTH: it reads
RealAntennas' runtime state through its PUBLIC API by reflection ONLY, and
re-derives link-budget figures from RealAntennas' PUBLIC static formulas and
constants (see local_docs/telemetry-mod/comms-uplink-design.md §4). It does NOT
link the RealAntennas assembly at build time, does NOT derive from RealAntennas
types, and bundles NO RealAntennas source or assets.
