gonogo

A mission control room for Kerbal Space Program.

Run your whole save from the browser: contracts, science, the administration building, launches, and live telemetry. Lay the widgets out the way you want, across as many screens as you want.

The main screen connects directly to your KSP install, so it runs on your own machine, not here. This page is just the front door.
